Get Involved

As a Medical Student

As a Student you can get involved in the following roles:
  • Apprentice
  • Consultant
  • Policy Paper Author
  • Advocate & Community Leader
  • Executive

APPRENTICE

For our first year operating, we will be running a pilot project using only students from Michael G. DeGroote School of Medicine (McMaster University) and Schulich School of Medicine (Western University). However, upon completion of our first term and subsequent quality improvements from our pilot, we will be recruiting medical students as apprentices and chapter leaders from all medical schools across Canada.

If you are a medical student from a Canadian medical school outside of McMaster or Western, and you are interested in getting involved as an Apprentice in upcoming terms, or are interested as a prospective chapter representative, please email us at mpmdapprenticeship@gmail.com

We're more than happy to put your name on the waitlist for the post-pilot operations and discuss ways to advance chapters of MP-MD Apprenticeship across the country.

If you are interested in participating as an Apprentice and you are a medical student from McMaster or Western, please fill out this form

CONSULTANT

Throughout our program term, many of our policy projects will touch upon areas of interest for medical students all over Canada. To ensure we represent the viewpoints of the medical student populace fairly and accurately, the MP-MD Apprenticeship will be hosting virtual focus group sessions on select topics open to medical students all over Canada, where we hope to solicit feedback and insight on current policies, proposed changes, and more.

To attend on our focus group sessions and policy talks, check out our calendar of events here. Follow us on social media, or add yourself to our mailing list to get all of our latest updates!

POLICY PAPER AUTHOR

Medical students often are highly experienced with research and paper-writing, so if you want to put these skills to good use on an advocacy/policy topic of your choosing, we're open to policy paper submissions year-round! These papers will be reviewed in a timely manner by our policy paper & research committee, and will be published on our website as well as our newsletter. Your paper will be able to be viewed by medical students across the nation and if the subject is relevant to one of our current projects/portfolios, we will bring it to the visibility of a relevant policymaker.

Further details to follow and keep an eye out for executive openings in our policy paper committee!

ADVOCATE & COMMUNITY LEADER

Want to have your initiative, organization, or advocacy efforts featured by the MP-MD Apprenticeship, visible to medical students and policymakers all over Canada?

If so, feel free to fill out the following form. For a featured article on our site or social media post about your changemaking and community building efforts.
